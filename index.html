New Homepage and Performance Improvements

ThemeJekyll got a new homepage displaying the most recently added themes. The themes page now shows thumbnails instead of full-size screenshots to improve performance on low-bandwidth connections.

May 9, 2017

Initial Launch

Hello, World! ThemeJekyll has been officially launched today, May 9th, 2017. As of launch, only 30 themes have been added to the database. Developers, if you would like to help ThemeJekyll grow (and get a backlink for yourself), please add your theme. Thanks!
